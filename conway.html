Conway's Game of Life

The Rules

Conway's game of life is a cellular automaton which "simulates" cellular underpopulation, overpopulation, reproduction, and society. That said, the rules for each generation of cells, according to Wikipedia, are the following:

  1. Living cells with less than two neighbors die.
  2. Living cells with more than three neighbors die.
  3. Living cells with 2 or 3 neighbors survive.
  4. Dead cells with exactly 3 live neighbors become living cells.
