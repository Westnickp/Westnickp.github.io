About Me and This Website

Welcome!

Welcome to my web page! If you're here, I'm guessing you are either a potential employer, a friend, or a stranger who's stumbled upon this page by accident. But I guess that's not too relevant: Whichever category you belong to, I'm hoping it'll be interesting enough to keep you around!

What is this website?

In short, I'm not really sure. I became interested in web design, and then, a few w3schools tutorials later, I wanted to make a website. If I had to describe its origins, I would characterize this website as a result of two impulses of mine: an impulse to create and an impulse to share.

I'm hoping that this site will act as a creative outlet for me. It has already served as an opportunity to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—and at the same time, to showcase those skills in a creative, tasteful, and fun way. I'll also be able to use it to render some of the other projects that I've completed (or intend to complete) visible—those usually more mathematics-based. That is, I see this site as an opportunity to bring to life some of the mathematical and physical processes that I find most interesting, to create a display for them that matches their beautiful innerworkings.

Creating seems to be intrinsically linked to community, and I am not above that rule. I want to use this website as a platform for sharing what I find interesting, beautiful, and fun. There are certainly other platforms in social media that are better suited to the sharing aspect, and I'm sure there are other people who have completed (likely with higher quality than mine) similar, if not identical, projects, but building these projects myself and sharing them through a medium that I've created feels more personal and communal. Doing so allows me to customize things so as to underscore what is most important to me.

Of course, not everything is so serious. I've also included some mini-games and math-memes just because they are fun. It's because of this eclectic gathering of jokes, games, mathematics, and other miscellaneous categories that just happen to draw my wandering attention long enough to earn a tab on this page that I have to say, in the end, I'm not really sure what this website is. But I've built it nonetheless, so here it is, and I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!
